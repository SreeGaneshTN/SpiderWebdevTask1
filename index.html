About the Game:

Fun Bubble Burst Game Using HTML Canvas in which bubble appear at random Places on the Screen.
The main Aim of the Player is to Burst the Bubbles Before they fill up the Screen.

Click to Start A new Game

Instructions:

>Bubble will be Generated at Random places in the Screen.
>Player has to click the Bubbles
>Game will be Over once the Bubble Fill up Major part Of the Screen.
>After Sometime Rock Bubble starts to Appear which Requires 5 click to Burst.
>Rate of Bubble Generation increase with Time.
>There will be 2 Special Power Liquid Luck and Thanos Gauntlet.
>Liquid Luck -> It reduces the Buble Generation Rate for 5 Secs.
>Thanos Gauntlet -> Swips out Half of the Bubbles.

Note: You can use the Special Power only 3 Times in the Whole Game!!!
